# CSI – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### CSI

#### CSI SB @ BVRITH

Computer Society of India (CSI) is a society for enthusiasts who are associated with computer technology. Today the CSI takes is the largest and most professionally managed association for IT professionals in India. The purposes of the Society are scientific and educational directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science and IT.

The Computer Society of India is a non-profit professional meet to exchange views and information learn and share ideas. The Society functions under the guidance of an Executive Committee.

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women CSI Student Branch was established in the year 2013 with Institute Membership No: M10017 Valid up to 30 th June 2021 . It was renewed with membership number 5024220017, valid up to 6th June 2024, and again renewed with the same membership number, valid up to 5th June 2029. Under CSI, Information Technology and Computer Science & Engineering department regularly organizes workshops, seminars, main project, mini project, and hobby project exhibition; blind coding, paper presentations skill Test, Java skill Test etc., and technical talks for the benefit of professionals and conducting continuing education and professional development programs for students.

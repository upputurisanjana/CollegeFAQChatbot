# PCS Facilities – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### PCS Facilities

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women created full fledged facilities to support and meet the needs of Physically Challenged Persons.

Following are the facilities:

- Lifts
- Ramps
- Washrooms for Divyangjan
- Wheelchair, EV Vehicle, and Wall Stick

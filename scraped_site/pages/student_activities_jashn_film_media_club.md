# JASHN – Film & Media Club – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### JASHN – Film & Media Club

#### JASHN: Where Requirements Meet Talent – Film and Media Club

JASHN: Where Requirements Meet Talent

– Film and Media Club

JASHN is the official Film and Media Club of BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women, created to bring together creative minds across all domains of cinema and media. The club provides a unified platform for dancers, writers, actors, singers, videographers, photographers, editors, and media enthusiasts to collaborate and transform ideas into impactful visual stories. JASHN plays a vital role in enriching campus culture through creativity, performance, and digital expression.

Introduction

JASHN – The Film and Media Club of BVRITHCEW is a student-led creative collective that nurtures talent in filmmaking, performing arts, and digital media. Guided by the belief “Where Requirements Meet Talent,” the club bridges institutional needs and student creativity by delivering high-quality content for college events, celebrations, and digital platforms. JASHN encourages artistic freedom while fostering teamwork, leadership, and technical excellence.

Objectives of JASHN

Creative Integration:

Bring together diverse talents required for filmmaking and media production under one collaborative platform.

Skill Enhancement:

Develop artistic and technical skills in acting, direction, scriptwriting, dance, music, cinematography, photography, editing, and digital storytelling.

Talent Utilization:

Ensure that creative requirements for college events, festivals, and promotions are met by in-house student talent.

Cultural and Artistic Growth:

Promote appreciation for cinema, arts, and media through events, screenings, and performances.

Digital Media Presence:

Strengthen the college’s social media and digital outreach through engaging visual and reel-based content.

Activities Conducted by JASHN

Chitraanga – Inter-College Short Film Competition:

Successfully organized an inter-college short film competition that encouraged storytelling, creativity, and technical innovation among young filmmakers.

Festive and Cultural Celebrations:

Planned and executed performances and media coverage for New Year celebrations, Christmas celebrations, and college festival events.

Movie Screenings:

Organized movie screening sessions to promote cinematic appreciation, analysis, and discussion among students.

Content Creation & Social Media Management:

Produced reels, posters, promotional videos, and managed social media handles for various college activities and events.

Media & Performance Contributions:

Actively contributed dancers, singers, actors, writers, photographers, videographers, and editors for college-level programs and creative initiatives.

Conclusion

JASHN stands as a creative backbone of campus life at BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women. By aligning institutional needs with student creativity, the club continues to inspire innovation, teamwork, and artistic excellence while building a strong and vibrant film and media culture within the college.

# Security – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

###### Security

### The precondition to freedom is security

As a coin have two sides, evils too reside in our society amongst good people. Thus we at BVRIT-H find it our prime duty to provide a safe and secure environment for all the students.

##### Some salient features of our security system:

- BVRITH provides 24/7 Security.
- Two teams of full-time security officers work in two shifts, patrol the campus.
- The campus is constantly monitored by 15 Closed-circuit televisions CCTV.
- A separate security officer is being employed at each hostel.

#### Highlights

- Special patrolling wing
- 24/7 Security surveillance
- Special investigation team

# Rotaract Club – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### Rotaract Club

The “Rotaract Club of BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women” does social service. The Rotaract Club exchanges ideas with leaders in the community, develops leadership and professional skills, and has fun through service. It is meant for young people who are aged 18 and older. It is a part of “Rotary Club International”, a global network of 1.4 million people with 46,000+ clubs.

# Research Ethics Committee – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

###### Research

### Research Ethics Committee

The Research Ethics Committee (REC) at BVRIT HYDERABAD plays a vital role in ensuring that research across all departments is conducted with integrity and in adherence to ethical standards. The committee is dedicated to safeguarding participant welfare, fostering responsible research practices, and ensuring that all scholarly work contributes positively to society while respecting individual rights.

In addition, the REC also functions as the Research Outcome Evaluation Committee (ROEC) , thereby extending its role to the overall promotion and evaluation of research quality within the institution.

- Review research proposals and protocols.
- Conduct ethics assessments and grant approvals.
- Monitor ongoing research and perform periodic audits.
- Offer guidance and training on ethical research practices.
- Investigate and address allegations of unethical conduct.

- Encourage faculty and students to engage in high-quality research.
- Create awareness about funding opportunities from government, industry, and international agencies.
- Motivate faculty for quality publications and collaborative research.
- Collect and maintain records on publications, patents, funded projects, consultancy work, and prepare periodic R&D reports.
- Organize seminars, FDPs, workshops, hackathons, and conferences on research and innovation.
- Align R&D activities with the institution’s vision, mission, and strategic goals.

With its diverse membership, representing academic, professional, and ethical expertise, the REC–ROEC ensures that all research activities at BVRIT HYDERABAD are not only ethically sound but also strategically impactful.

# NATURE CLUB – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### NATURE CLUB

It is said that nature is the best teacher. It teaches us survival of the fittest and nourishes us in all aspects. nature has endowed us with incredible assets like, biodiversity and natural resources. The conversation of these assets has become need of the time. It’s our responsibility to leave this planet in better for the future generations.

Nature Club aims at including an appreciation of nature among students and encouraging them to participate in a variety of conservation initiatives.

Nature Club will create awareness among the students, faculty and staff about nature and related issues.

#### Events:

Various events conducted under Nature Club so far:

- Awareness session on Save Water Save Earth
- Workshop on Cloth Bag Making
- Eco Friendly Ganesh Making
- World Environment Day

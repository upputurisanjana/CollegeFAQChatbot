# NIRF – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### NIRF

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a methodology released annually by the Ministry of Education in India to rank higher education institutions. BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women was ranked by NIRF in Engineering and Innovation.

# Information Technology – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

###### under graduate

### Information Technology

##### We have placed around 55 students in various Multinational Companies such as Capgemini, CSC, Accenture and many more.

Information Technology has an immense impact on one’s life in modern day world in all axes. Hence it creates a huge demand in educating youth in this discipline. The testimony is in front of our eyes seeing the exponential growth in this sector in the past one and a half decade. Education in Information Technology imparts the knowledge through which the needs of users within an organizational and societal context can be met. IT department in BVRITH has occupancy of 120 seats educating everyone with the latest available technology.

#### Course Highlights

### B.Tech.

- A four year degree program offered by BVRIT-H.

- Aimed to provide an ability to design and analyze any IT network comprising of any prevalent technology within realistic constraints.

- Trains a student to function on multi-disciplinary teams, formulate, and solve engineering problems and designing comparing solutions for the cumulative development.

- Gives the student opportunity to pursue a career in various domains including Networking Specialist, IT engineer etc.

# R & D Center – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

###### Research

### R & D Center

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for women aims to advance the knowledge and practice of modern evidence-based impactful research through the R&D centre. The purpose of the centre is to enable and strengthen the research aspiration among faculty, scholars, students, to enhance research innovation, academic excellence, real-world problem-solving skills, knowledge creation and dissemination though various research opportunities. It aids faculty to exhibit their research verdicts via journal publications, patents, conferences, books, etc., The R&D centre keeps exploring new research avenues for the faculty and students, keeping the research conduit of the institution stable.

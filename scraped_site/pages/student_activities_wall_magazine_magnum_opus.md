# Wall Magazine – Magnum Opus – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### Wall Magazine – Magnum Opus

### “Nothing sublimely artistic has ever arisen out of mere art … There must always be a rich moral soil for any artistic growth. — G.K. Chesterton

Creativity is one of the highest transmissions of love. At BVRITH, students’ imagination and creativity is nurtured and given wings to soar to the sky. They create beautiful and meaningful pieces of art to express their emotions. These are showcased by “MAGNUM OPUS”, the College Wall Magazine.

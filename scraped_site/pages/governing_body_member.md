### Sailing towards success

BVRITH has become synonymous with women empowerment through quality technical education and social success for young engineers. The intensive teaching methodologies prepare aspiring women to reach greater heights in their careers. The very foundation of BVRITH was laid to make the institution fulfill the ambition of every woman. A few personalities who are continuously working hard for BVRITH to make it a great success, holds the Governing Body panel. The governing body panel comprises of distinguished people who care for the cause of women engineers.

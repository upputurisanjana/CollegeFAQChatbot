### Introduction

RAKSHANA – Your Shield is the Women’s Safety Cell at the Women’s EngineeringCollege, dedicated to ensuring the safety, security, and well-being of all female students, faculty, and staff. Our mission is to create a secure and supportive environment whereeverywoman can thrive without fear of harassment or discrimination.

### Objectives of RAKSHANA

1. Safety and Security: Establish a safe and secure environment for women on campus.
2. Support and Counseling: Provide confidential support and counseling for individuals facingharassment or discrimination.
3. Awareness and Education: Educate the campus community about women’s safety, genderequality, and cybercrime prevention.
4. Grievance Redressal: Address and resolve complaints related to harassment, abuse, andcybercrimes effectively and sensitively.

### Activities

Workshops and Seminars: Conduct regular workshops on self-defense, legal rights, gendersensitization, and cyber safety.

- Counseling Services: Offer confidential counseling to support those in need.
- Helpline: Provide a 24/7 helpline for immediate assistance and reporting of incidents.
- Safety Audits: Perform regular safety audits to identify and mitigate potential risks on campus.
- Awareness Campaigns: Organize campaigns to increase awareness about women’s rights, safety, and cyber safety.
- Collaboration with SHE Team: Partner with the SHE Team for enhanced safety measures and joint awareness initiatives.

RAKSHANA – Your Shield is committed to ensuring a safe and empoweringenvironment for all women on campus.

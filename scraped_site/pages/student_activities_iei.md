# IEI – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### IEI

The Institution of Engineers India (IEI) is the national organization of engineers in India It has more than one million members in its 15 engineering disciplines across 125 centers and chapters located in India and around the world. It is the world’s largest multi-disciplinary engineering professional society in the world, and acclaimed to have pioneered non-formal education in engineering.

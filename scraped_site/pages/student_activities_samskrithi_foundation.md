# Samskrithi Foundation – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

### Samskrithi Foundation

Samskruti Foundation has been striving towards nation building by promoting and nurturing Indian Culture, traditions and quality of life through various programs of outreach to different sections of the society.Samskruti Foundation (SF) is promoting our Cultural Identity by involving the intelligentsia of the society in inspiring, educating and encouraging people towards upholding Indian culture, traditions and values through various programs and activities.

Vision

To be a National Resource centre for Culture.

Mission

To preserve, protect, and encourage the practice of Bharatiya culture and ethos through research, education, training, and dialogue.

Objectives

- To serve the society as a whole in every manner without any distinction of Caste, Class or Creed and eliminate of all types of social evils.
- Create social consciousness and a civic sense among people, promote the harmony & integration of various diversities.
- Focuses on creating the value-based ecosystem in young minds through Cultural leadership Centers (CLC).
- To create the Man Power Resources ecosystem of (voluntary workers, full-time and part-time) for the outreach.
- Undergoing the Research activities through Samskruti Adyayana Kendra(SAK) to deep dive on the challenges related Socio, Cultural, Political, Spiritual & Economical aspects.
- Highlighting the significance, importance & contributions of ancient Indian dignitaries in the field of Science & Technology, Economics, Public Administration & Other areas.

## Internships

Students get Internship opportunities right from their 1st year till the final year. Students were paid as high as 1.1 lakh/month stipend during their Internship. Some of the companies that offered internships are Microsoft, Amazon, Qualcomm, Flipkart, Target, Qlik, etc apart from various other opportunities with a duration generally between 2-6 months. Internships come through the campus or Off- Campus with the college guidance. Many internship opportunities are getting converted to full time offers, but even otherwise the industry exposure and experience is helping the students to grab the other job opportunities.

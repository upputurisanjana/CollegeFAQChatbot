**Placements**

## Training & Placement Process

A Diagnostic Test is conducted to assess the foundational skills of incoming first-year B.Tech students and to identify areas that need improvement. It evaluates students’ strengths and weaknesses across key domains such as Mathematics, English, Psychometric Testing, Quantitative Aptitude, and Mental Ability. These insights help faculty customize their teaching strategies and provide focused support, ultimately enhancing the overall learning experience.

Basic Programming Skills enable students to leverage the power of computers to develop innovative solutions, automate routine tasks, and solve complex problems. Learning these skills opens up a wide range of career opportunities in technology and other fields, while also strengthening critical thinking, logical reasoning, and problem-solving abilities.

The Vishnu Student Success Centre (VSSC) offers a holistic support system that promotes student growth and development. Its services include personalized tutoring, academic assistance, and career mentorship, all tailored to individual student needs. In addition, the Centre conducts workshops on wellness, personal development, and confidence-building to ensure well-rounded support for student success.

Women in Software Engineering (WISE) is a distinctive initiative that runs alongside the regular academic curriculum. It aims to prepare women engineering students for the global tech industry by equipping them with up-to-date technical skills. The program is delivered in two levels, where students are trained in the latest technologies, making them industry-ready and highly competitive.

Web Application / Gaming Development Skills (using Python) – Python is a powerful and versatile language used for both web application and game development. In web development, frameworks like Django and Flask allow for rapid creation of secure, scalable applications through built-in tools for routing, templating, and database integration. In game development, libraries such as Pygame and Panda3D support the creation of interactive 2D and 3D games, including graphics, sound, and user input. Python’s simplicity, vast libraries, and strong community support make it ideal for developing, testing, and deploying projects across platforms.

Intellectual Learning in Engineering Applications (ILEA) is designed to promote mindset transformation and self-reflection among students. It focuses on personality development and building self-awareness related to career success. Students are guided to identify their strengths and areas of improvement to become placement-ready. The program also highlights the importance of gratitude and uses psychometric assessments to help students gain deeper insights into their personal and professional growth.

Aptitude Training aims to enhance a student’s natural abilities in areas such as logical reasoning, quantitative skills, and problem-solving. This training helps improve performance in aptitude tests and prepares students for various competitive and academic challenges.

Entrepreneurship Awareness initiatives encourage students to view self-employment and business creation as viable career paths. These programs provide the necessary knowledge and skills to develop an entrepreneurial mindset, introduce participants to the start-up ecosystem, and promote innovation, initiative, and self-reliance.

Competitive Coding helps students sharpen their problem-solving abilities, improve coding efficiency, and develop logical thinking. Through regular practice in timed coding challenges, students gain a strong grasp of data structures and algorithms. These skills are essential for technical interviews and real-world software development. The program includes three levels of training in Competitive Coding.

Industry Programs / Internships / Mentorship s – The college has multiple collaborations with industry partners, allowing students to pursue certifications that lead to internships and mentorship opportunities. These programs bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry expectations, enhance practical skills, and provide exposure to current trends and real-world challenges.

Alumni Support plays a vital role in supporting both the institution and its current students. They contribute through mock interviews, motivational and technical talks, career guidance, networking opportunities, and job referrals. The alumni also serve as ambassadors of the institution, helping build its reputation and supporting its growth and development.

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (BVRITH), the youngest offspring of Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES) founded by Chairman Sri K V Vishnu Raju in 2012.

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (Autonomous) was established in the year of 2012 with the clear Vision & Mission to empower the women to aspire for a better career. College was accredited by NAAC with Grade ‘A’ in the year 2020 and granted UGC autonomous status from AY 2023-24 for a period of 10 Years. The Institute is duly approved by All India Council for Technical Education, Government of Telangana and is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad. UG Programs – B.Tech. (EEE, ECE, CSE) were accredited by NBA. College offers 4 UG Programs in B.Tech. EEE, ECE, CSE and CSE (AI&ML) with an intake of 660 and 3 PG Programs in M.Tech (Data Sciences) with an intake of 18, M.Tech (VLSI Design) with an intake of 12 and M.Tech (CSE) with an intake of 12.

In spite of being a new college, by following different unique practices the college became one of the first choices among all affiliated colleges under JNTUH for the aspiring students who want to elevate their talent more than the academics.
